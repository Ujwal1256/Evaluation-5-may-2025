Must visit Places in Nagpur


  1. Deekshabhoomi

  2. Hazrat Baba Tajjuddin Dargah

  3. Nagpur Ramtek Temple

  4. Shree Ganesh Mandir Tekdi

Key Travel Tips


  • Visit between October and March for pleasant weather.
  • Carry Sunscream and water while sightseeing.
  • Use local transport like auto-rickshaws and municipality bus service
  • Try local deliccies like Saoji food and Tari Poha
  • Respect local customs and religious sites
